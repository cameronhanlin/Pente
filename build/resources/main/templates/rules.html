How to take a turn:

  • Players take turns placing one piece on the board. Click where you would like your piece to be set.
  • The pieces are placed on the intersections of the lines, rather than inside the squares.
  • The first play is always in the center of the board.
    • The first Player is chosen at random.
    • Click anywhere inside the board to start play.

How to win:

  • The object of the game is to either get five pieces in a row (vertically, horizontally or diagonally) or capture five of your opponent's pairs.
    • The first Player to reach either objective, wins immediately.
  • Captures are made by surrounding two of your opponents' pieces (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) with two of your pieces, with one on each side.
    • A player's pieces cannot be captured by moving in-between two of their opponent's existing pieces.
    • Multiple captures when placing a single piece is allowed.
